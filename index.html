    Our Infro

    Afrotrade Corporation Limited is a diversified trading and distribution company strategically positioned at Plot 13969, Chandwe Musonda Road, Light Industrial Area, Lusaka, Zambia. The company operates across two vital sectors – energy and agriculture – supplying high-quality petroleum products, industrial oils, chemicals, and agricultural commodities to both domestic and regional markets. Fully licensed by the Energy Regulation Board (ERB), Afrotrade ensures compliance, safety, and quality in all its energy-related operations. On the agricultural front, the company plays a key role in strengthening food security and supporting value chains that link farmers, industries, and markets across Zambia and beyond
